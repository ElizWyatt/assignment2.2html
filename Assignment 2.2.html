About Me

When it comes to my Real Estate career, my motto and mission is, "My Customer Comes First". Whether I am assisting in the sale of your house or helping you find your next dream home, my focus is to take the stress out of the transaction and make the process as worry-free as possible. Having studied engineering at Georgia Tech, I appreciate the importance of paying attention to details and making all of the pieces come together to solve a problem. This type of focus enables me to manage all of the details of a real estate transaction starting with the offer all the way through a successful closing. Through my extensive experience as a sales professional, I have fine-tuned my negotiating and communication skills. As a result, whether you are buying or selling I can help you negotiate the best price the market will support as well as help negotiate any additional details of the contract. I enjoy working with both sellers and buyers. It is so gratifying to me when I can help a client sell a home quickly at a fair market price. I also especially enjoy helping customers find that Florida dream home they are looking for. I look forward to working with you soon.
